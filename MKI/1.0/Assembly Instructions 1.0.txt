Assembly Instructions for Floata-Buddy MKI 1.0 (Non Funcional)

Required Items:
- 3d Printer
- PLA or PETG Filament
- Elastic Strap

1.) Put ".stl" file into slicer software for 3d printing
2.) Use gird supports with 15% infill
3.) 3d Print items
4.) Remove items from build plate and slot the top peice into the bottom piece
5.) Slot elastic strap into holes on the bottom piece of the model
6.) Strap around wrist
